    Welcome to PRO PRESSURE CLEAN CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

    PRO PRESSURE CLEAN provide domestic and commercial construction services, we are a unique Pressure Washing and building firm providing all aspects of building work and project management. A company that specialises in conversions, extensions, maintenance and refurbishments of any size and type of property, including kitchen and bathroom makeovers. All our time served tradesmen are available covering groundwork, drainage, joinery, plastering, plumbing, electrics, roofing, decorating, brick and stonework. Our experienced team are reliable, tidy and above all trustworthy, and will complete your project from start to finish with all work guaranteed and completed to your satisfaction.

    PRO PRESSURE CLEAN business is built on recommendation; satisfaction achieved through hard work and happy customers who realise we offer quality workmanship at a competitive price.

    Internal Building Work
