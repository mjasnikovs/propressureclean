    Some words about us

    PRO PRESSURE CLEAN Services is fully committed to providing a professional and efficient cleaning service at all times using the latest equipment and machinery to produce excellent results in conjunction with Health & Safety standards. We hold CSCS cards and are SAFE Contractor Approved and CHAS Accredited.

    Our vehicles have mounted pressure washers and hose reels thus meaning we don't mess about unloading and loading equipment, minimizing lifting procedures and manual handling.

    Our vehicles are equipped with first aid and eye wash stations and fire extinguishers.

    Based in Ipswich, Suffolk in the UK, PRO PRESSURE CLEAN are a pressure washing and windows cleaning company that specialises in all aspects of Domestic and Commercial pressure washing, window and Power cleaning.

    PRO PRESSURE CLEAN specialise in the professional cleaning and restoration of all hard surfaces in both the domestic and commercial market. Surfaces such as Block paved Driveways, walls, roofs and Patios can be Cleaned and transformed to look as good as new. Using our specialist, unique pressure washing cleaning equipment, we can quickly and effectively remove ingrained dirt, moss, algae, weeds, cement, paint & oil staining using the power of water and all necessary chemicals!
